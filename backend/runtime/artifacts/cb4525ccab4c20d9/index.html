About Sonai Dairy

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Sonai Dairy has grown from a small family farm to one of the leading dairy producers in the region. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and animal welfare has been the cornerstone of our success.

We work with over 500 local farmers who share our values and dedication to producing the finest milk using traditional methods combined with modern technology.

Today, we offer a wide range of dairy products that meet the highest standards of quality and taste, serving millions of happy customers across the country.
