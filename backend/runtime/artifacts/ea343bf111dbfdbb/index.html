Our Story

Founded in 1985, Sonai Dairy has grown from a small family farm to one of the region's most trusted dairy producers. For over three decades, we've been committed to providing the highest quality milk and dairy products.

Our cows are pasture-raised and grass-fed, ensuring that every product we create is rich in nutrients and flavor. We combine traditional methods with modern technology to maintain the purity and freshness of our products from farm to table.

Today, we serve thousands of families and businesses across the country, never compromising on our values of quality, sustainability, and community.

About Sonai Dairy

Our Story Since 1985

Founded with a vision to provide pure, nutritious dairy products to families across the nation, Sonai Dairy has grown from a small family farm to a trusted name in the dairy industry.

We maintain the highest standards of quality and hygiene throughout our production process. Our cows are raised in stress-free environments and fed natural diets to ensure the best possible milk quality.

Today, we serve over 1 million happy customers and continue to innovate while staying true to our traditional values.
