About Sonai Dairy

With over 50 years of excellence in dairy farming and production, we take pride in delivering the highest quality products to millions of families.

Our Story

Founded in 1970, Sonai Dairy began as a small family farm with a commitment to quality and sustainability. Today, we've grown into a leading dairy producer while maintaining our core values of purity, freshness, and environmental responsibility.

Our state-of-the-art facilities combined with traditional farming methods ensure that every product bearing the Sonai name meets the highest standards of quality and taste.
